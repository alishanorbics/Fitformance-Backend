About Us

The science is undeniable: exercise is one of the best measures we have to combat pain, degeneration, and disease.

Whether recovering from an injury, preparing for surgery, or living with a chronic medical condition – our Post Rehab Training programs are here to keep you moving. We utilize exercise as our tool to strengthen and stabilize the body, delivering our clients the confidence to exercise without fear or hesitation.

Our staff is made up of Certified Medical Exercise Specialists. We hold degrees in the health sciences field and have completed over 1,000 hours of hands-on training. We are skilled at designing programs specific to each individual based on their strengths, weaknesses, and fitness goals.

You will not find our exercise programming anywhere outside of our walls except for in our clients’ hands.
